Gyroscope & accelometer in mobile devices

Accelometer is a sensor that detects how the unit moves in the form of acceleration and gyroscop sensor detects how much it rotates in degrees. These sensors are mostly used to detect landscape and portrait mode on the device, but has lots of other applications to applications and games.
